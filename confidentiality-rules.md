# Privacy Policy – Alakastats (Android)

**Last updated: February 18, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

Alakastats is an application dedicated to Pokémon team and statistics analysis.  
Protecting your privacy is a priority.

This Privacy Policy transparently explains how the application handles user information.

---

## 2. Data Collection

**Alakastats does not collect any personal data.**

Specifically:

- No personal identification data (name, email address, device identifiers)
- No location data
- No health data
- No financial information
- No usage data sent to external servers
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies
- No tracking technologies

---

## 3. Data Storage

All data you enter into the application (teams, statistics, notes, analyses, etc.):

- Is stored **locally on your device only**
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Is never shared with third parties
- Is accessible only by you

You may delete this data at any time by:

- Uninstalling the application
- Clearing the application's data through Android system settings

---

## 4. Advertising

Alakastats:

- Displays no advertisements
- Uses no advertising SDKs
- Shares no data with advertising networks

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The application:

- Does not use analytics services
- Does not use tracking services
- Does not transmit any data to third parties

---

## 6. Android Permissions

Alakastats does not request any sensitive permissions.  
If standard system permissions are required by Android for basic functionality, they are never used to collect or transmit personal data.

---

## 7. Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, no external processing occurs.  
The security of locally stored data depends on your Android device’s protection mechanisms (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

The application does not collect any data and can be used without providing personal information.  
No data related to children is collected.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised accordingly.

Alakastats is a 100% local application, with no data collection, no advertising, and no information sharing.
